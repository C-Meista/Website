About Us

Welcome to Chainzz – Where Quality Meets Style

At Chainzz, we believe a neck chain is more than just an accessory – it’s a statement. Founded by Michael Senn, a passionate designer and solo entrepreneur, Chainzz was born from a deep appreciation for craftsmanship, minimalism, and timeless style.

Our Story

What started as a personal journey to find the perfect neck chain evolved into a mission: to create high-quality, durable, and beautifully designed chains that elevate any look – without the insane markups.

After months of testing materials and prototypes, Michael launched Chainzz to deliver premium chains that blend urban edge with refined elegance.

Each piece is carefully curated or designed with an obsessive focus on detail, comfort, and finish. Whether it’s a bold Cuban link or a sleek rope chain, we ensure that what you wear around your neck reflects strength, confidence, and authenticity.

Why Chainzz?

  • Premium Materials – Only top-grade stainless steel, sterling silver, and gold plating that resists tarnishing and lasts for years.
  • Craftsmanship You Can Feel – Every chain is hand-inspected and quality-checked before it reaches you.
  • No Middlemen – We sell directly to you, giving you luxury-level quality at honest prices.
  • One-Man Brand, Big Vision – Michael personally oversees every aspect of the business, ensuring a unique and personal experience.
